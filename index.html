What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste (E-waste) refers to discarded electronic devices such as smartphones, laptops, tablets, and other gadgets. These items often contain toxic materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium which are harmful to both people and the environment.

Why Should We Recycle E-Waste?

Environmental Protection: Prevent harmful chemicals from polluting soil and water.
Recover Valuable Materials: Extract gold, silver, copper, and more from old electronics.
Reduce Waste: Minimize landfill usage and conserve resources.

How You Can Help

  • Donate or sell your working old devices.
  • Use certified recycling points in your area.
  • Spread awareness among your friends and family.
  • Join clean tech movements and e-waste drives.
